How ReRead Works

Thanks for your interest in ReRead! ReRead is in its early stages and is still being developed. This means there will be hiccups and hurdles to overcome as we go!

The technology and business strategy are evolving continuously as we work towards making ReRead a sustainable business. The current workflow is outlined here:

  1. Request a book.
  2. Our team will search charity shops for your book. This might take some time, but we’re developing new scanning technologies to speed up the process!
  3. If we find your book, you will be alerted via email.
  4. If you choose to proceed, a £1 reservation fee is required. We will then request the charity shop to reserve the book for you.
  5. Collect and enjoy your newly sourced sustainable and charitable book!
