How ReRead Works

Thank you for your interest in ReRead!

ReRead is in its early stages and continues to evolve. As we develop, expect some hiccups and hurdles along the way!

Our technology and business strategy are continuously improving as we strive to make ReRead a sustainable business. Here’s how our current process works:

  1. Request a book: Let us know what book you're looking for.
  2. Search: Our team will search charity shops for your book. This might take some time, but we're developing new scanning technologies to speed up the process.
  3. Notification: If we find your book, you will be alerted via email.
  4. Reservation: If you choose to proceed, a £1 reservation fee is required. We will then ask the charity shop to reserve the book for you.
  5. Collection: Pick up and enjoy your newly locally sourced sustainable and charitable book!

Get involved and help make ReRead a reality by requesting your first book today!
