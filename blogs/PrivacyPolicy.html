Privacy Policy for ReRead

10th October 2024

  1. Introduction

    At ReRead, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your information.

  2. Information We Collect

    We collect the following types of information:

    • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.
    • Wishlist Data: We collect information about the books you add to your wishlist by referencing Google Books, including links to Google Books entries for each book.
    • Search Data: When you perform searches on our platform, we log your search terms and interactions.

  3. How We Use Your Information
    • Wishlist Notifications: We use the information in your wishlist to notify you when a book is found in a participating charity shop.
    • Search Results: Your searches are used to show relevant books and charity shop locations.
    • Improvement of Services: We use aggregate data from users to improve our services and enhance the user experience.
    • Marketing: With your consent, we may send marketing emails or notifications about new features and promotions.

  4. Sharing Your Information

    We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

    1. Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate the platform, such as hosting providers.
    2. Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may disclose your personal information to comply with legal obligations or protect the rights and safety of ReRead and its users.

  5. Data Security

    We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

  6. Data Retention

    We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

  7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    ReRead uses cookies and similar technologies to track user activity and improve the functionality of the platform. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

  8. Your Rights
    1. Access and review your personal information.
    2. Request corrections to inaccurate data.
    3. Request deletion of your account and associated data.

  9. Children's Privacy

    ReRead is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

  10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and you will be notified of any significant updates.

  11. Contact Information

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us at [hello@re-read.co.uk].
