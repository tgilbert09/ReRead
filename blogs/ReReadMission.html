ReRead's Mission

8th August 2024

The books you want,
from the places you love.

ReRead was started to serve book lovers who prefer sustainability and charity over convenience and instant delivery. We believe that many readers would rather purchase second-hand books from local charity shops than buy from online stores. It is our mission to confirm this belief and determine how best to serve these customers.

ReRead also aims to introduce new technologies to charity bookshops, an industry typically not at the forefront of technological innovation. Our goal is to identify and meet the needs of these shops, enhancing their ability to generate donations and revenue, which in turn supports amazing causes.

If you are excited by the ReRead mission, help us build a sustainable reading community by requesting your first book today!
