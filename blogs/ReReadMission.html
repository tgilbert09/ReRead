ReRead's Mission

The books you want,
from the places you love.

ReRead was started to serve book lovers who are willing to trade convenience and instant delivery for sustainability and charity. ReRead makes the leap of faith assumption that people would rather buy second hand from local charity shops over online stores. It is ReRead's mission to discover if these people exist, and if so how they can best be served.

ReRead also intends on bringing new technologies to charity bookshops, an industry that is not usually known for being at the cutting edge of technology. ReRead aims to identify and serve the needs of charity bookshops to enhance donations and revenue, enabling them to further support amazing causes.

On a personal note, it is my dream to work on a sustainable, community based, tech project and I would love to create something that other people love using.

If you are excited by the ReRead mission, help us start a sustainable reading community by requesting your first book today!
