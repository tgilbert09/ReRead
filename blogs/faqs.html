Frequently Asked Questions

11th October 2024

Why are there multiple versions of the same book in my Wishlist search results?

ReRead uses Google Books, which has a vast database that includes different editions, formats, and releases of the same title. As a result, you might see multiple listings for the same book in your search results.

Do I need to add every edition of the book to my Wishlist?

No, there's no need to add multiple versions of the same book. ReRead will check for close matches based on the title and author, so adding just one version is enough! Feel free to choose your favourite book cover or edition, and we’ll take care of the rest! :)

Disclaimer: While ReRead aims to connect you with the books you're searching for, we don't track specific editions or formats. The book you’re notified about may differ slightly (e.g., cover or edition) from the one you added to your Wishlist.
